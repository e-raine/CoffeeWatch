About Cafe Libro

Located in the heart of Mintal, Cafe Libro has become a favorite spot for students, freelancers, and coffee enthusiasts. It features a variety of espresso drinks, pastries, and a relaxing atmosphere perfect for spending a few hours working or socializing.
